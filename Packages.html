    Mount Bromo, Jawa Timur.
    Mount Bromo is one of the active volcanoes in Indonesia. The mountain which has a height of 2,392 meters above sea level is a mainstay destination for East Java. Mount Bromo stands proudly surrounded by a caldera or sea of sand covering an area of 10 square kilometers. Tourists who visit Mount Bromo will be greeted by beautiful views.
